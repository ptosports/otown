otown events — Support

otown events is an iMessage game for discovering things to do around Orlando and Central Florida. Swipe through local activities, send the game to a friend, and see which ones you both want to do.

How to play

  1. Open a conversation in Messages and tap the apps icon, then choose otown events.
  2. Enter your name and swipe: right to like an activity, left to pass.
  3. Tap send — your friend gets the same set of activities to swipe.
  4. Once they finish, the message shows the activities you both liked. Tap any match to open its website and start planning.

FAQ

Does my friend need the app? Yes — like other iMessage games, both people need otown events installed to play.

How often does the activity list change? It refreshes automatically each week, so the deck stays current and shows events happening within about a month.

Do you collect my data? No. See our Privacy Policy.

An event's info is wrong or a link is broken. Let us know and we'll fix it.
