Privacy Policy — Weltita

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Short version: Weltita does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

Information stored on your device

The only thing the app saves is the display name you type, so your friends can see who sent a game. It is stored locally on your device (in the app's own settings) and is never transmitted to us or anyone else. You can change or clear it at any time.

Your game choices

When you finish swiping, your picks are encoded into the iMessage you send. That message travels through Apple's Messages service directly to the friend you chose — it never passes through any server we control, because we don't operate one. We cannot see your choices, your matches, or your conversations.

Activity list

The app downloads a public list of Central Florida activities from a static file hosted on GitHub. This is a one-way download that contains no personal information about you. As with any internet request, the host may see standard technical data such as your IP address; we do not receive or retain it.

Links to other sites

Activity cards include a "More info" link that opens the event's own website in your browser. Those websites are operated by third parties and have their own privacy practices, which we do not control.

Children's privacy

Weltita is not directed to children under 13 and collects no personal information from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
